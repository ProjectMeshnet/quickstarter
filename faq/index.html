F.A.Q.

» Will I recieve the items from the lower tiers if I buy a higher tier?
Yes. By buying a higher tier, we will include the items from the tiers below the tier you purchase.
» When will my items be delivered?
The delivery schedule is as follows: the $1+ tier will be delivered within 3 business days of purchase; the $25+ tier will be delivered within a month of purchase; the $50 tier will be delivered in full within 2 months, or as scheduling permits for the videochat; and the $125 tier should be tenatively delivered within 3 months of purchase. Note that all of these timeframes may be subject to change at any time.
» I live outside the US. How much extra will shipping be for my item?
We will happily estimate the cost of shipping your item to you, please contact us at support@meshwith.me.
» I would prefer my poster to not be trifolded. How much extra would it be to have it shipped in a tube?
In the US it will be $5 extra. Please contact us at support@meshwith.me if you reside outside the US and we will estimate the cost of shipping your items to you.
» What will come with my meshnet router?
We will include a router flashed with OpenWRT & Cjdns with peering for Cjdns provided by Dan Ryan. The router will have 5 10/100mbps ethernet ports, and be capable of 802.11n connectivity in the standard Wifi band of 2.4ghz.
» Where can I see the code that this selfstarter has paid for?
All code written to add OpenWRT support will be attached to the Cjdns OpenWRT bug, and if we meet our goal and move on to our stretch goal of Windows support, that code will be made available here. Additionally, all code will be made available under the current license of cjdns.
» When will Cjdns work on Windows?
If we meet the $350 goal for this Selfstarter, we will move on to our strech goal of $3250, which will pay for Windows support for Cjdns.
» Where is the money going?
OpenWRT Support would take 10 hours at $25 USD an hour, which is $250 USB in total. $75 is going to be for rewards such as T-shirts and routers. $25 for incidental costs such as (but not limited to) payment processing fees, overruns in shipping expenses, and overruns in CJD's time estimate. For payment processing, figure on 2.9% +30 cents. Around 4% all said and done. That'd be $14 in payment processing fees in total.
» Who will be signing the posters?
A few of the people from the Seattle Meshnet. Most likely it'll be Finn Hertzfeld, Dan Ryan, and Matthew Bentley.
» What risks does your project face?
If everything goes wrong in writing OpenWRT support for Cjdns, you will still recieve the rewards that came with your donation.
» What if my question hasn't been answered?
Please contact us at support@meshwith.me and we will answer any and every question that you have.
